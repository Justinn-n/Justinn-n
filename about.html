About the project

Supporting students with smarter career planning

This project is part of C240 AI Essentials & Innovations. Our team, Team 3, created Singapore Career Pathfinder to help students explore future career options in a simple and practical way.

The chatbot supports students by connecting their diploma, interests, and skills to realistic job paths. It also gives them helpful ideas about salary range, employer expectations, and the skills they may still need to build.

Our goal is to make career planning less confusing and more personal, especially for students who are preparing for internships or graduation.
